## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer retention and churn for a subscription-based business using Power BI.  
The objective is to identify churn patterns, retention trends, customer cohorts, and lifetime behavior, and present insights as recommendations for a real-world business scenario.

## Objectives
- Clean and organize customer subscription data  
- Analyze churn rates and overall retention trends  
- Perform cohort analysis based on tenure, contract type, and customer attributes  
- Identify customer lifetime patterns and revenue impact  
- Determine key churn reasons and retention drivers  
- Present insights with actionable business recommendations  

## Tools Used
- Power BI  
- Power Query  
- DAX  

## Dashboard Summary
The Power BI dashboard provides:
- Overview of customers at risk and churn metrics  
- Churn analysis by tenure, contract type, payment method, and demographics  
- Customer lifetime value and revenue-related insights  
- Identification of high-risk customer segments  

## Key Insights
- Customers with shorter tenure exhibit higher churn rates  
- Month-to-month contracts contribute significantly to churn  
- Certain payment methods and service types show higher churn risk  
- Long-term customers generate higher lifetime value  

## Recommendations
- Strengthen onboarding and engagement for new customers  
- Promote long-term subscription plans to improve retention  
- Implement targeted retention strategies for high-risk customers  
- Encourage stable and automated payment methods  

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates how Power BI can be used to analyze customer churn and retention data, enabling data-driven decisions to improve customer loyalty and business performance.
